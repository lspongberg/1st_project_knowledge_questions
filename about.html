The Ways of Knowing

The video above gives a great explanation of the difference between first and second order knowledge questions.

  • Make it matter


    Your topic should be significant & engaging.

  • Make it open-ended


    Ask a question that has multiple legitimate answers.

  • Make it a 2nd Order KQ


    Ask a question that asks how we know about the topic.
